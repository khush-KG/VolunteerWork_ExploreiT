Tree Plantation

Why Tree Plantation is Important

Tree plantation is important for various reasons. Trees help to combat climate change by absorbing carbon dioxide from the atmosphere. They also provide oxygen and improve air quality. Trees help to prevent soil erosion and improve water quality by filtering rainwater. They also provide habitat for wildlife and contribute to biodiversity.
